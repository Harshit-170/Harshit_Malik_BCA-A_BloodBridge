Why Blood Donation Matters

Save Lives

Every unit of blood you donate can save up to three lives. Your contribution matters.

Support Hospitals

Regular blood supply is critical for surgeries, emergencies, and cancer treatments.

Community Impact

Blood donation builds a stronger, healthier community ready for any crisis.

The Blood Donation Process

1. Registration

Provide basic details and confirm your eligibility before donation.

2. Health Check

Undergo a quick health screening to ensure you’re fit to donate.

3. Donation

Relax as you donate blood under the supervision of trained staff.

4. Refresh & Recover

Enjoy refreshments and take a short rest post-donation.
