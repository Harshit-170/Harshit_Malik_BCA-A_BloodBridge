Overview

BloodBridge is a platform that directly connects people in urgent need of blood with verified donors nearby. Whether you're donating or requesting, the process is quick, transparent, and built to save lives faster than traditional methods.

How It Works

1. Register

Sign up as a donor or requestor using your name, location, and contact details.

2. Submit a Request

If you need blood, fill out a form with required blood group, location, and urgency level.

3. Donors Notified

Nearby donors with the matching blood group are instantly notified via the BloodBridge system.

4. Direct Communication

Donors can contact the requester directly, without delays from banks or third-parties.

5. Donate Safely

Meet at a verified hospital or blood donation center to complete the donation safely and legally.

6. Get Acknowledged

Donors receive acknowledgment and appreciation badges via their BloodBridge profile.

Why BloodBridge?

Traditional blood banks may not always have the right blood type at the right time. BloodBridge solves this by mobilizing local donors fast — reducing response time and saving more lives. We eliminate middle layers and give power to the community.
