About Us

At BloodBridge, we are driven by a single mission — to save lives by connecting those in need of blood with generous donors in real time.

In moments of urgency, every second counts. That's why we've built a platform that bridges the gap between blood seekers and donors, bypassing delays from hospitals or blood banks, ensuring help reaches where it's needed most — fast.

Founded with compassion and powered by technology, BloodBridge simplifies the process of blood donation and requests. Whether you’re a donor looking to make a difference or someone in urgent need of blood, our platform offers a seamless way to connect, communicate, and coordinate.

We believe in the power of community, humanity, and technology to save lives — one drop at a time.

Frequently asked questions

Who can donate blood on BloodBridge?
Anyone aged 18–65, in good health, and weighing at least 50 kg can register as a donor. Additional eligibility may apply based on medical history.
Is donating blood through BloodBridge safe?
Yes. All donations are conducted through certified medical professionals following strict safety protocols. BloodBridge simply connects donors with recipients in real-time.
Do I get paid for donating blood?
While you don't receive direct payment, BloodBridge rewards dedicated donors with gift vouchers, exclusive prizes, and achievement badges after certain milestones. It's our way of appreciating your life-saving contributions!
How often can I donate blood?
Men can donate every 3 months, and women every 4 months, provided they meet the health criteria at the time of donation.
Can I request blood urgently on BloodBridge?
Yes. You can post an urgent blood request, and nearby compatible donors will be notified instantly via SMS and email.
Is my personal data safe on BloodBridge?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and never share your data without consent. Privacy and security are our top priorities.
